Log-in to the Business Analytics Report Editor

Figure 2‑1: SmartSuite Reports Menu

To log into the Report Editor:

  1. Launch any web browser and enter the URL of the SmartSuite Web Client (http:// http://bhs-dhs/webclient).
  1. Log in to the SmartSuite Web Client with your user credentials
  1. Select Business Analytics from the Reports menu. The Business Analytics page displays.
  1. Click Report Editor. The Report Editor opens.
