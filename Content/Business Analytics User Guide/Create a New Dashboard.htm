Create a New Dashboard

You must be logged into the Report Editor. Use these steps to create a new dashboard.

  1. From the Report Editor page, click Create New, then select Dashboard.
  1. On the bottom of the page, click the Properties tab, and enter a title for your dashboard page in the Page Title text box. The name you entered appears on the top left corner of the dashboard. This name helps you identify the page if you want to edit, copy, or delete it later.
  1. Click the Templates tab to choose a dashboard layout. A blank dashboard with the layout you selected appears.
  1. Click the Themes tab to choose a theme for your dashboard. The theme you selected is applied to your dashboard.
