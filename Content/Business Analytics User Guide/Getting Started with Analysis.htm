Getting Started with Analysis Reports and Dashboards

Introduction

The Report Editor is a fun, easy to use, web-based design environment where you can analyze data, and build integrated dashboards to share business intelligence solutions with others in your organization.

The topics covered here give you an overview of the reports and dashboards you create with the Report Editor, to help you became familiar with the look and feel of the application.

Quick Tour of the Report Editor

If you use file management tools or any web browser, you should feel right at home with the SmartSuite Business Analytics Report Editor. To familiarize yourself with the different pages and controls of the Report Editor, let us take you through a quick tour.

Log in to the Business Analytics Report Editor

 

  1. Launch any web browser and enter the URL of the SmartSuite Web Client (http://bhs-dhs/webclient).
  1. Log in to the SmartSuite Web Client with your user credentials
  1. Select Business Analytics from the Reports menu. The Business Analytics page displays.

  1. Click Report Editor. The Report Editor opens.

Home Screen

The first thing you see after you log in to the console is the Home screen, which serves as a starting place for the Report Editor and all of the tasks you do with it.

Home Indicator

Shows the name of the person currently logged in to the Report Editor. Clicking the arrow next to the name lets you log out of the Report Editor.

Browse Files

Brings you to the Browse Files window, where you can locate your files using the Browsing and Files panes, and manage them using the Actions pane.

Gives you the option to create a new Analyzer Report or Dashboard.

Documentation

Opens the SmartSuite Business Analytics user guide.

Recents

Shows a list of your most recently opened files. Clicking on the star next to a recently opened file adds it to your Favorites list.

Shows a list of your favorite files for quick access.
