Editing a Calculated Measure in the Report

You can edit a calculated measure that has been created in a report.

  1. Click the down arrow next to a calculated measure in the Layout panel, select User Defined Measure from the menu, and then select Edit Calculated Measure. The Edit Calculated Measure dialog box appears. Optionally, you can double-click the calculated measure in the report to open the Edit Calculated Measure dialog box.
  1. In the Name field, edit the name of your calculated measure.
  1. In the Format field, edit how you want the results of your measure to appear in your report. Optionally, edit the number of decimal places for the results.
  1. In the right panel, edit the formula for your calculated measure. You can write the MDX statement, or you can use the list on the left to drag measures into the right panel. You can also use the symbol buttons below to help edit your statement, or just use your keyboard to write the expression.
  1. Select the Calculate subtotals using measure formula checkbox to use this calculated measure when adding up subtotals in your report. Clear this checkbox to not use this formula when calculating subtotals.
  1. Click OK to apply your calculated measure to your report. When you save your report, your calculated measure will also be saved with your report. Click Cancel to close the dialog box without applying your changes.
