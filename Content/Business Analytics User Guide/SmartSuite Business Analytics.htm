SmartSuite Business Analytics Report Editor Overview

The SmartSuite Business Analytics Report Editor (Report Editor) Suite is a single, comprehensive platform from which you can access, manipulate, visualize, and analyze your data.

The Report Editor Web-based Design Tools include:

  • Analyzer
  • Dashboard Designer

Analyzer

Analyzer helps you filter and visualize data to make informed business decisions. You can use Analyzer to filter data, add query parameters, configure drill-down links, apply conditional formatting, and to generate hyperlinks. You can also create scatter chart, heat grid, and multi-chart visualizations.

Figure 1‑1: Analyzer

Dashboard Designer

Choose layout templates, themes, and content to create visually attractive dashboards that help decision makers recognize critical knowledge at a glance. Embed a wide variety of content in the web-based display, Analyzer visualizations, graphics, charts, web pages, even collaborative content. Users can personalize dynamic dashboards to reflect their preferences.
