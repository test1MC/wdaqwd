Equipment Malfunction Summary Report

The Equipment Malfunction Summary Report summarizes the information provided in the Equipment Malfunction Report (see section 9.17 Equipment Malfunction Report) by listing a count and total duration for specific alarm categories. Note that alarms will not appear in this report if they have not been configured to be part of specific equipment categories.

The following selection criteria are available:

  • Start Date and Time
  • End Date and Time

Figure 9‑20: Sample Equipment Malfunction Summary Report

The following information is included in this report:

Column

Description

Equipment

The device category that the alarms are associated with.

Occurrences

The number of alarms which fall under that device category.

Total Duration

The total time that all devices within the equipment category remained in an active malfunction.
