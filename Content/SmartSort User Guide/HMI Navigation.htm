HMI Navigation

The HMI screens are divided into two primary sections:

  • Title/Navigation Bar – Displays various buttons/menus used for navigation through the system, logos, date and time, workstation name, and current user name.
  • Process Window – Displays the conveyor system and the status of individual components (e.g. motors, photo eyes, etc.). Many detailed graphics screens are used to show the separate conveyor sections of the system. Several textual status screens display statuses and statistics for various elements of the system as well.
