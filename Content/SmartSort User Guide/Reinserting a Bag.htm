Reinserting a Bag

When a bag arrives at a BRP terminal with a security status of either BHS Unknown or ISD Unknown, the user can reinsert the bag into the BHS. Bags will be marked for reinsertion when the Reinsert button on the BRP is clicked.

A bag marked for reinsertion will be conveyed to the furthest downstream BRP terminal and will continue onto the reinsertion line automatically. If a bag marked for reinsertion stops at a BRP terminal, the BRP will indicate that the bag has previously been marked for reinsertion.
