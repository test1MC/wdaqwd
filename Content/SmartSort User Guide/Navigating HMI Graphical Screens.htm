Navigating HMI Graphical Screens

To access one of the detailed graphics screens, from the Overview screen, hover the mouse over the desired portion of the system. A white outline will be displayed around the area where the mouse is hovering. Left-clicking with the mouse will open up a detailed graphics screen for the portion of the system that is displayed within the outline. Clicking on any of the individual sections will zoom in on an individual graphical screen for that section. Many of the detailed graphical screens will have arrow buttons that can be used to view adjacent portions of the system.

Figure 3‑2: Sample HMI Graphics Screen

Clicking the Zoom Out button in the bottom right corner of the screen will zoom back out to the Overview screen for that portion of the system. Right-clicking anywhere on the screen also zooms out to the Overview screen.

Each of the detailed graphics screens is also accessible via the Graphics menu in the Title/Navigation Bar.
