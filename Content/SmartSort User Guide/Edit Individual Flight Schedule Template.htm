Template Management – Edit Individual Flight Schedule Templates

The template flight management view allows modifications to the Flight Schedule Template in an Excel-like grid. Content can also be copied to and from Excel. This allows for easy edits as well as bulk editing. The following is a sample of what the Edit Individual Flight Schedule Template screen looks like.

Figure 4‑29: Flight Schedule Template - Template Flight Management View

To return to the Template Management List of Flight Schedule Templates screen, select the List button on the upper left corner of the screen.
