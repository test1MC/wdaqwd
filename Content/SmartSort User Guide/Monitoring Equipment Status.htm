Monitoring Equipment Status

The status of system equipment is indicated via color in the HMI. Many devices in the system (e.g. conveyors, diverters, etc.) are configured to change color depending on the status of the equipment (e.g. Jam Fault, E-Stop Fault, etc.). This allows a user to quickly see which parts of the system are running normally and which parts are experiencing a fault condition.

These color conventions are also used by the equipment status screens (see section 7.1.2 Viewing Equipment Pop-Up Windows).
