Bag Transit Time Report

The Bag Transit Time report includes information on how long bags spend in transit in the system.

The following selection criteria are available:

  • Start Date and Time
  • End Date and Time

Figure 9‑15: Bag Transit Time Report

The following information is included in this report:

 

Column

Description

Transit Area

Each transit area covers a portion of the overall BHS. Transit time is a measure of the duration of time it takes for a bag to travel through these “transit areas” of the system.

Min Time

The minimum amount of time any one bag spent travelling through the transit area (hh:mm:ss).

Max Time

The maximum amount of time any one bag spent travelling through the transit area (hh:mm:ss).

Avg Time

The average amount of time bags spend travelling through the transit area (hh:mm:ss).

Total Bags

The total number of bags that were tracked from the beginning to the end of the transit area.
