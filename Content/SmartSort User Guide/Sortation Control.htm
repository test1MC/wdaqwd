Sortation Control

The Sortation Control portion of SmartSort is used to manage how bags are sorted to the various sort destinations in the system (piers) SmartSort allows the user to customize the settings that determine how bags will be sorted.

Here is a very high-level overview of sortation control:

  1. The sortation settings are configured by an administrator (see the SmartSort System Administration Manual as well as sections 4.2 Configuring Carrier Make-Up Units and 4.3 Flight Schedule Management for details).
  1. The Virtual to physical mapping is configured (see section 4.1 Virtual to Physical Mapping for details).
  1. Flight schedule templates are imported and/or created (see section 4.3.3 Flight Schedule Template Management).
  1. Every morning the flight schedule template(s) are consolidated by SmartSort and activated for the current day.
  1. The system uses the flight schedule, the virtual to physical mapping, as well as the configuration settings to determine where bags will be sorted for each flight.
  1. The flight schedules can be modified and managed as required (see section 4.3.2 Active Flight Schedule Management).
