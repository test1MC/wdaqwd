KPIs Pane

To view KPIs, they must be enabled using the Settings pane (see section 8.5.1.2.1 Settings Pane). Once enabled, users can click on KPI icons throughout the system, which displays KPI Detail Cards. KPI Detail Cards provide statistical information in the KPI Pane (see Figure 140). KPI Headlines can view viewed by hovering over KPI icons throughout the system. To stop viewing KPI information, users can select the Clear All button on the top of the KPIs Pane.
