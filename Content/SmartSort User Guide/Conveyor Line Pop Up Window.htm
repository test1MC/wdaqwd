Conveyor Line Pop-Up Window

The Conveyor Line Pop-Up window is displayed by clicking on a conveyor group from a Graphics screen. The Conveyor Line Pop-Up window contains:

  • Current Status of All Motors (Conveyors) in the Line.

Figure 7‑11: Conveyor Line Pop-Up Window

Users can access an individual belt’s pop-up by clicking the device’s respective status.
