HMI Display Conventions

The following topicssections describe the color-coding conventions used by the equipment status screens. Since multiple statuses may apply to a single device at the same time, the status colors are prioritized. Only the status color with the highest priority will be displayed. A priority of 1 indicates that the status is of the highest priority, while a priority of 10 indicates a lower priority. Note that not all of the statuses and colors may be required in the system.

The color coding conventions can also be found in the Legend screen, accessed via the Utilities menu in the Title/Navigation Bar.
