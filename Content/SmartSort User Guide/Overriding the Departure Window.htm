Overriding the Departure Window

The flights that are displayed on the Bags En Route Dashboard are determined by the departure window. Flights for which the Estimated Departure time (not Scheduled Departure Time) is within the departure window are displayed on the dashboard. By default, the departure window starts at 35 minutes before the current time and 0 minutes after the current time.

Figure 7‑36: Bags En Route Dashboard - Departure Window

The default departure window can be adjusted by changing the number of minutes in the Before Current Time and After Current Time fields at the top of the Bags En Route Dashboard.
