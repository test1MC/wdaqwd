Dimensioner Performance Report

The Dimensioner Performance Report provides a summary and time-based accounting of the performance of the bag dimensioning devices upstream of the CBIS area.

The following selection criteria are available:

  • Start Date and Time
  • End Date and Time
  • Interval (Minutes, Hours, Days, Weeks, Months, or Years)
