HMI/Workstation

The following sections outlineoutlines thecommon HMI and workstation issues, and the probable causes and remedies for those issues.

Colors Not Appearing on HMI (Wireframe)

If this occurs on all HMI workstations, the OPC Server isn't communicating with the PLC controlling that section:

  1. Confirm that the PLC communication is OK by pinging the PLC Ethernet card.
  1. Contact Brock Solutions Support to restart the components on the main HMI server to try to restore connectivity.

If this only occurs on certain HMI workstations, individual HMIs may need to be restarted:

  • Restart individual HMI workstation.
Some Standard HMI Functions Not Working

For example, pop-ups or menus are not functioning.

Background processes have been interrupted or crashed:

  1. Confirm that the date and time in the HMI header are updating.
  1. If there is no activity, the HMI application must be restarted from its shortcut.
  1. Use the shortcut Alt+F4 to exit the HMI.
  1. Double-click the shortcut icon on the desktop to relaunch the HMI.
HMI Not Displayed on the Correct Screens

Incorrect configuration:

  1. Check that the Windows monitor settings are configured as a rectangle (no gaps).
  1. Press the Home button or use the desktop shortcut to restore the HMI to its original location.
Workstation Screens Are Blank

Monitor cables are loose or unplugged:

  1. Check at the rear of the monitor and the computer that all cables are secure.
  1. If reattaching cables, ensure that they are returned to their original location. If monitors are moved or resized, restoring their settings is tedious and difficult.

Incorrect monitor resolution:

  • Confirm monitor resolution and change as appropriate.
