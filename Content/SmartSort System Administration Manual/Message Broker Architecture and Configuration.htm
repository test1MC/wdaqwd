Message Broker Architecture and Configuration

Bag Pipeline

The bag pipeline is expected to be configured such that:

  • BSMs are received from Sabre for AA.
  • BSMs are received from Arinc for All other airlines.
  • BPMs are sent to Sabre for AA bags
  • BPMs are sent to Sabre for all other airlines bags
  • Flight Messages are received from AA (BOSS) via WebSphere MQ for AA flights.
  • Flight Messages are received from AODB for all other airlines
  • BSMs are sent to SITA BagMessage to be delivered to several airports to support sortation, loading and other local operations (and BPMs could be received back).
