Bag Transactions

At each critical stage throughout the life of a bag in the baggage handling system, the system will record transactions for easy tracking of what happened to the bag. If the bag is associated with an IATA barcode, the location of the event will be recorded in that barcode's Last Seen Location. The following transactions will be recorded:

  • BSM Message Received (also BSM Change and Delete)
  • BPM Sent (confirmation that a BPM has been sent will be logged every time a BPM is sent to host)
  • Bag Measured at Dimensioner
  • Bag Scanned at CBIS ATR
  • Pre-ISD Message (Bag hand off to ISD Machine)
  • ISD Update (Bag Security Status)
  • CBRA Arrival (Reason for sending to CBRA)
  • Bag Released at MEC
  • Bag Scanned at Sortation ATR
  • Logical Reject at Sortation ATR
  • Bag Successfully Diverted/Merged
  • Bag Failed to Sort
  • Bag Status Update (e.g. bag is lost in tracking, or unknown bag is detected)
  • Bag Photograph Taken at ATRs
