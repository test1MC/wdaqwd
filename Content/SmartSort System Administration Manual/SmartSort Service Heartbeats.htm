SmartSort Service Heartbeats

All SmartSort services are configured to generate heartbeat messages with the IPC2OPC service installed on the DHS. The heartbeat follows the standard echo-response pattern. When running correctly, the service reads a value and echoes the value back. If the IPC2OPC service detects that the echo has not been received, an alarm will be shown on the HMI and the associated bubble on the Communication Status screen will turn red.
