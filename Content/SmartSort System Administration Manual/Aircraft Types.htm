Aircraft Types

Aircraft Types configuration is used to configure the various aircraft types in the system, including any overrides to the default Open Period, Close Period, or Late Period(see section 2.4.2 Carrier Settings and 2.4.10 Virtual to Physical Mapping Configuration) that may be required for that aircraft.
