CBRA/CBIS

The following sections outlines common CBRA/CBIS issues, and the probable causes and remedies for those issues.

The following outlines the most common CBRA/CBIS issue along with the probably cause and potential remedy for the issue.

High Number of Unknown Bags Sorted to CBRA

Poor bag hygiene:

  1. Check the Photo Eye Cell Tracking report to isolate trouble areas or devices.
  1. Inspect and improve bag hygiene.

Tracking photo eyes misaligned or not working properly:

  1. Check the Photo Eye Cell Tracking report to isolate trouble areas or devices.
  1. Physically inspect problem photo eyes for correct operation.

Incorrect belt speeds:

  • Use belt tachometer to ensure belts are running at recommended speeds.

Malfunctioning belt encoders:

  1. Check the Photo Eye Cell Tracking report to isolate trouble areas or devices.
  1. Visually inspect belt encoder connections.
  1. Check belt encoder resolution (if adjustable).
  1. Ensure encoder is secured properly.

For information about generating reports, see SmartSort User Guide Chapter 9: Understanding Web Client Reports.
