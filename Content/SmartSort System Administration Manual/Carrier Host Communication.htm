Carrier Host Communication

FIDS (Flight Information Departure System)

In order to associate flights to destinations (i.e. Makeup Units), the system needs a list of all the available flights for a predefined period of time, typically 24 hours. There will be two FIDS interfaces, one for American Carriers and the other for all other carriers (from DFW).  These FIDS interfaces will provide the SAC with up-to-date information about all flights departing from the airport.

The SAC will comply with the following FIDS interfaces:

  • AA BOSS Interface (all American Carriers flights and will include makeup unit)
  • DFW AIDX Version 9.1 (all other Carriers)
