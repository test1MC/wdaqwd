Host Connections (BSM/FIDS)

The following sections outline outlines the common host connection issues, and the probable causes and remedies for those issues.

BSM Connection Alarm

Partial connection to the BSM host

  • One of the redundant BSM connections has failed. This will not affect system performance, but contact the BSM provider for further assistance.
No Bag Data Arriving for Sortation

Complete disconnection from the BSM host

  • Contact the BSM provider for further information and assistance. The Smart Connect Message Broker Service will continue to try to connect as long as it is running.
  • During this time, bags will sort in fallback mode based on information that is available.

Services on the SAC are not running.

  • Restart the Bag Message and Smart Connect Message Broker Service services.
BPMs Aren't Being Sent

The BHS operators will not notice this problem, but it may be reported to them.

BPMs for an Carrier have been configured to be turned off:

  • Check the 2.4.2 Airline Settings screen (see section 2.4.2 Carrier Settings)to confirm that BPMs are enabled for the appropriate Carriers.

A service has encountered an error:

  • Restart the Bag Message Service on the SAC.
  • Some queued BPMs may be lost, but processing will resume.

The Carrier Host is not accepting messages from the BHS (rare):

  • Contact BPM receiver for help.
  • Queued BPMs will be held by the Smart Connect Message Broker Service until the queue fills up or the service is stopped.
