Workstations

The Workstations can be treated like standard Windows computers. To shut down or reboot the computer, select Shut Down from the Start Menu and choose the appropriate option from the dialog box. The Start Menu may be hidden by default. Move the mouse to the bottom of the screen or press the Windows key on the keyboard, and the Start Menu appears over top of the HMI windows. The HMI can be shut down by pressing Alt + F4 prior to shutting down the workstation.

To power on the Workstations, press the power button on the front of the computer. It reboots into Windows, automatically log in as the Operator, and start up the HMI application.
