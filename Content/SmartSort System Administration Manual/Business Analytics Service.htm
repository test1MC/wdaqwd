Business Analytics Service

This service hosts the Business Analytics dashboards, reports, and report editor. Usage of these items is described in the SmartSuite Business Analytics Report Editor User Guide.

If this service is stopped, the Business Analytics dashboards, reports, and report editor will be unavailable. The database used by the Business Analytics service will continue to gather data even if this service is stopped.
