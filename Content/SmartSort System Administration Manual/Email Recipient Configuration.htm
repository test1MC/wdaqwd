Email Recipient Configuration

The system administrator can configure and manage users so that they receive email alerts upon specified events. The Email Accounts setup screen is used to establish these groups. This screen displays a list of all existing email accounts. Email addresses can be added and deleted.

Figure 2‑2: Email Accounts Setup Screen

Clicking on an email address in the Email Accounts list displays the information for that email account in the Email Account Details section.

To add a new Email Address:

  1. Click Add New Email Account. The Account Settings section is populated with blank fields.
  1. Enter the desired email address and the account owner's name in the corresponding fields.
  1. In the Email Group Membership section, select the groups to which the email recipient should belong. (For information about Email Groups, see section 2.1.4 Email Group Configuration for details).
  1. Click Add Email Recipient to save the new Email Account.
  • Clicking Cancel or navigating away from the page cancels any unsaved changes.

To edit an existing Email Account:

  1. Select the Email Account from the Account List. The Email Account's details display in the Account Settings section of the screen.
  1. Edit the Email Account's information as necessary.
  • An email account can be enabled or disabled by using the Account Enabled checkbox.
  1. Click Save Changes to save the changes to the Email Account.
  • Clicking Cancel or navigating away from the page cancels any unsaved changes.

To delete an existing Email Account:

  1. Select the desired email account(s) to be deleted by checking the corresponding boxes on the leftmost side of the Account List section of the screen.
  1. Click Delete Selected Email Accounts and confirm the deletion on the confirmation dialog.
  • To cancel the Delete operation, click Cancel.
