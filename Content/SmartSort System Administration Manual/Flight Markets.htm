Flight Markets

Flight markets (Domestic, International, Transborder) can be configured with unique default Open Times, Late Times, and Close Times. These times are used to calculate the time-based makeup unit assignments in the flight schedule. The Flight Markets screen is used to manage these default times.

Figure 2‑16: Flight Markets Screen

To add a new flight market:

  1. Click Add New Flight Market. The Market Details section on the right side of the screen is populated with blank fields.
  1. Using the fields in the Market Details section, enter the appropriate flight region information and enter the desired Open/Late/Close period in minutes.
  1. Click Add New at the bottom of the screen to save the new flight region. Clicking Cancel cancels the flight market creation.

To edit an existing flight market:

  1. Select the desired flight market from the Market List. Note that this list can be filtered by typing in the Filter field at the top of the list. Clicking the flight region in the list causes that flight market's details to be displayed in the Market Details section.
  1. Using the fields in the Market Details section, edit the flight market's settings as required.
  1. Click Save Changes to save the changes to the flight market. A confirmation dialog is displayed.
  1. Click OK to the save the changes. Click Cancel to abort the save.

To delete an existing flight market:

  1. Select the checkbox next to the appropriate Flight Market located in the Market List.
  1. Click Delete Selected Flight Markets at the top of the screen. A confirmation dialog displays.
  1. Click OK to delete the flight market(s). Clicking Cancel aborts the deletion.
