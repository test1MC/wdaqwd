FIDS Messages

There are two types of FIDS providers to the BHS: 

  • AA Flight Hub: Receives current AA flight updates up to six hours in advance for active flights, downloads future flight schedules on demand via the Web Client.
  • MDAD AOIS: Receives flight updates for non-AA flights, sends pier assignments back for use on the FIDS displays at the sort destinations.
