Anti-Virus Software

A computer virus is a program that causes the computer to behave in undesired or unexpected ways. Viruses can infect a system and produce no visible effects for long periods of time. Once a system is infected, it can be difficult to completely remove the virus. Viruses can infect a system in many different ways, including over the network, from an infected USB memory stick or Floppy disk, or through email.
