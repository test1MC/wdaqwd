DHS Communication to PLC is Intermittent or Unstable

Kepware having difficulties communicating with the PLCs:

  1. Confirm that PLC communication is functional for the rest of the system (HMI and MEC).
  1. Shutdown Kepware on the DHS. If the services are functioning correctly, Kepware will automatically restart within 30 seconds.
  1. Open a Kepware Quick Client to verify that the server has connectivity to the PLC tags. Sometimes leaving the Quick Client open helps with initial connection problems.
