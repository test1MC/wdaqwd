BPM (Bag Processed Messages)

BPM messages return data to the Carrier host systems to inform them of events that have occurred to particular bags. The SAC will generate BPMs for the following actions (assuming the bag is tracked with an IATA tag):

  1. A bag is scanned at any ATR, in screening or sortation.
  1. The security status of a bag becomes clear by an ISD.
  1. A bag is released from an MEC.
  1. A bag arrives in CBRA.
  1. A bag arrives at its final sort destination.

The BHS will send AA BPMs over a TCP-socket-based connection. BPMs for other Carriers can be configured to be sent to Arinc Bag Link upon request.
