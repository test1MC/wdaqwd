Flight Schedule Activation

Flight schedules are used to associate a flight with a destination for bags that are checked in for that flight (e.g. flight pier, carousel, make-up unit, etc.). These flight schedules are managed using the SmartSuite Enterprise Web Client. Each day, a flight schedule is automatically loaded (activated) by the SAC.

The SAC is responsible for activating flight schedules daily. At a configurable time (typically 02:00), the SAC takes flights from all the appropriate Flight Schedule Templates and copies them to the Active Flight Schedule.

The flight schedule is typically configured to hold three days' worth of active flights with estimated/actual departure times for yesterday, today, or tomorrow.

The Active Flight Schedule and the Flight Schedule Templates can be viewed and edited using the SmartSuite Enterprise Web Client Application (see the SmartSort User Guide for details).
