Computer Equipment Maintenance

The server room has a controlled environment so equipment should be relatively clean. Use a non-static cloth for minor dust removal from equipment. Use compressed air to clean excessive dust from equipment. The servers may have a filter accessible under front cover that can be cleaned by removing it and clearing it with compressed air.
