Preventative Maintenance

Preventative maintenance is an essential practice to ensure the system is running at peak performance levels. As a part of manual maintenance, the SAC, DHS, and HMI servers should be rebooted at least once or twice a year, or as many times as recommended per your IT Department's recommended practices. This ensures that the servers continue to function at their proper performance levels.
