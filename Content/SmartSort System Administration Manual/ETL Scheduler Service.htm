ETL Scheduler Service

This service triggers the ETL Service to perform its tasks periodically. The type of task being performed by the ETL Service determines the frequency that it runs its tasks.

If this service is stopped, the Business Analytics dashboards and reports will be unable to provide up-to-date information.
