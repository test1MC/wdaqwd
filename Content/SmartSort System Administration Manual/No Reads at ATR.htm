No Reads at ATR

Note that a No Read still means that data is flowing from the ATR to the BHS control system.

Bags have no readable tags:

  1. Confirm that bags have only one readable tag.
  1. Inspect bag hygiene.

ATR Heads are dirty:

  • Make sure the heads are clean or blown off with compressed air to get the optimal read rate.

ATR controller faulted on the HMI

  • Reboot ATR controller.
